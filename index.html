A little bit about me:

Hello everyone! Thank you for taking the time visit my website. My name is Morrigan and I am a home baker living in Murroe, County Limerick. I have over 2 years experience in baking and have been loving every minute of it. I can cater to any occasion, and love to take on any opportunity to get creative with my baking. I specialise in cakes and cupcakes for events such as birthdays, engagements, Christenings, or just about anything you wish to mark a special day for. If you would like to see more of my work or get in touch, please click on the Facebook or Instagram links at the bottom of my page and send me a message. Please remember to include the date you are looking for when booking.
Morrigan xx
